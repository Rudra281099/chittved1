Founder's Story

Rudraksh Sharma

Chittved was born from a simple but powerful observation: most young Indians are confused, stressed, or stuck — and have nowhere to turn that truly understands them.

Rudraksh Sharma, a psychology practitioner and educator, founded Chittved to bridge the gap between academic psychology and everyday life. Having worked with students across hundreds of schools, he saw that the real need wasn't just therapy — it was practical psychology.

The vision was clear: make psychology accessible, warm, and genuinely useful for adolescents, young adults, and families navigating a complex world.

"I don't want psychology to be a last resort. I want it to be a daily tool for clarity."

— Rudraksh Sharma, Founder

What is Psychology Circle?

A community built for clarity

Psychology Circle is a structured, mentor-guided community where students don't just learn about psychology — they use it to navigate their own lives.

🗣️
Weekly Peer Discussions
Every week, members gather (online/offline) to discuss real situations — relationship stress, exam anxiety, career confusion, family dynamics. No judgment. Just curiosity and care.
🧑‍🏫
Mentor-Led Sessions
Experienced psychologists lead and guide each session. They introduce frameworks, challenge assumptions, and help the group go deeper — safely.
📖
Case-Based Learning
Learn psychology through stories — not just theories. Real (anonymized) cases are discussed, analyzed, and understood together. Psychology suddenly makes sense.
❤️
Emotional Support Space
When you need to talk — this is where you come. A circle of people who get it, guided by professionals who ensure it stays healthy and constructive.
